Hi, I’m Kari, a front-end developer. I am passionate about turning great ideas into reality.

I believe in making beautiful, interactive, responsive web sites that serve the user.

This passion and belief has been nurtured through my study and practice of visual arts and film. I graduated with honours from Sheridan College's Media Arts Program in 2003. After graduation I began working in production and coordination capacities in independent film. In 2011, looking to branch out of film, I began my undergraduate degree at the University of Toronto. I graduated with honours in the summer of 2015 having majored in Sociology and minored in Linguistics and Buddhism, Psychology and Mental Health. My studies further instilled my love for sharing ideas and helping others and led me to pursue a career in web development.

Moving forward, I plan to take my wide breadth of experience, knowledge and skills and find a position that is collaborative, engaging and challenging.
